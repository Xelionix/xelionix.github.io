About Us

Xelionix GP is an indie game company dedicated to creating unique and engaging gaming experiences. We are passionate about pushing the boundaries of gameplay and storytelling, striving to deliver games that resonate with players on a personal level. Our team of talented developers and designers work tirelessly to bring innovative concepts to life.

Our Games

At Xelionix GP, we pride ourselves on our diverse portfolio of games. Each title we develop is a labor of love, carefully crafted to provide players with immersive worlds and memorable experiences. From survival challenges to civilization-building adventures, our games offer something for every type of gamer. Our flagship game, Harsh Habitats, is a testament to our commitment to quality and creativity.
